Blanc Manger Retro

To start the game, each player draws 10 white cards cards.
First round: The facilitator (the scrum master on this first first round) draws a question card and reads it aloud. out loud. This is a fill-in-the-blank sentence.
Question and answer cards are generally neutral. It's the combination of the two that makes for an explosive cocktail.
The other players play an answer card to complete complete the sentence. The completed sentences are read by the facilitator.
Sentences that make sense (we're in retrospect after all ☺ ) are explained. hindsight all the same ☺ ) are explained.
Two points are then awarded: - 1 point for the quirkiest or funniest sentence - 1 point for the most constructive or relevant The winner with the funniest sentence becomes facilitator for the next round.
